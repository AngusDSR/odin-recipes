Ingredients

  • 1 cup short grain rice
  • 100g vegetarian mince
  • 50g frozen peas
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2cm ginger, diced
  • 2 -4 garlic cloves, diced
  • 1 tbsp mirin
  • 2 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp miso paste
  • 500ml vegetarian bouillon
  • 1 tbsp potato starch or cornflour mixed with 1 tbsp of cold water
  • 1 block of silken tofu, cut into even cubes

To serve

  • 2 spring onions, thinly sliced
  • Toasted sesame seeds
  • Chili oil

Steps

  1. Rinse rice then drain water twice before covering in 2 cups of water and a pinch of salt for 30 minutes.
  2. Before finishing the sauce begin cooking the rice in a rice cooker or in a saucepan by bringing to a bowl, stiring then cooking on the lowest possible heat until fully steamed - around 15 minutes.
  3. Heat a pan, add 1 - 2 tbsp neutral cooking oil then fry onion until soft.
  4. Add garlic, ginger, mince and frozen peas, fry until soft and everything in the pan is cooked.
  5. Add mirin, sesame oil, oyster sauce and miso paste, followed by the bouillon.
  6. Once everything is combined, add the potato or cornflour solution andstir through, then turn down to low heat.
  7. Carefully add the tofu cubes, spooning the sauce and mince over and between them, allowing them to settle into the sauce as it thickens.
  8. Add rice to bowls and then spoon the tofu and sauce over them.
  9. Sprinkly the spring onions, sesame seeds over the top and stir in chili sauce as desired.
