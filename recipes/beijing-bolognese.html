Ingredients

  • 120g dried wheat noodles or spaghetti
    • if using spaghetti: 1 tbsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 100g vegetarian mince
  • 1 medium onion. finely diced
  • 1 medium carrot, finely diced
  • 1 - 2cm ginger, diced
  • 2 - 4 garlic cloves, diced
  • 100ml Tomato passata
  • 1 tbsp shaoxing wine
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp sweet bean sauce
  • 2 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp gravy powder
    • or 1 tbsp each of vegan bouillon and flour
  • 1 -2 tsp caster sugar

To serve:

  • 2 spring onions, thinly sliced
  • Toasted sesame seeds
  • Chili oil

Steps

  1. Heat a pan, add 1 - 2 tbsp neutral cooking oil, then fry onions and carrots on a low heat until soft, around 10 minutes.
  2. Start a large pan of water boiling for the noodles. If using spaghetti, add bicarbonate of once boiled then spaghetti as this will take around 10 minutes to cook.
  3. Add garlic and ginger then the vegetarian mince.
  4. Add the shaoxing and light soy sauce, stiring until it absorbs into the mince.
  5. Add the passata, oyster sauce and sweet bean sauce, and sugar letting it thicken slightly.
  6. Add the gravy or bouillon mix, along with extra water if needed.
  7. Stir in dark soy sauce and turn off heat once depending on desired consistency is reached.
  8. Salt the boiling water and, if using spaghetti add the bicarbonate of soda.
  9. If using wheat noodles, add these to the water now and cook according to pack instructions.
  10. Drain noodles and divide between boils serving sauce over these then top with spring onions, sesame seeds and chili oil.
